Football is the world's most popular sport.

It is played with 11 players per team and the goal is to score more goals than the other team.

The FIFA World Cup is the most prestigious tournament in football.

Famous players include Lionel Messi, Cristiano Ronaldo, and Neymar.

Football has millions of fans around the world.

It brings people together, regardless of nationality or language.
